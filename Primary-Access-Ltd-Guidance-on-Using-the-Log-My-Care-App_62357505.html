Staff Centre : Primary Access Ltd Guidance on Using the Log My Care App

Created by Mark Bryant on Aug 06, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Primary Access Ltd's guidance on effectively using the Log My Care app. This app is designed to streamline your daily tasks and ensure the highest standard of care for our clients. Below are some key points to help you navigate and utilize the app efficiently.

Key Points for Using the Log My Care App

1. Always Use the To-Do List

The To-Do List is your primary tool for managing daily tasks. Make sure to:

  • Check the To-Do List Regularly: It contains all the tasks assigned to you for the day.

  • Prioritize Tasks: Complete tasks based on urgency and importance.

  • Mark Tasks as Completed: As soon as you complete a task, mark it off in the app to keep your list up-to-date.

2. Use the Clients Tab to Filter Tasks

To manage your workload effectively and reduce clutter:

  • Access the Clients Tab: Navigate to the Clients tab to view tasks specific to individual clients.

  • Filter by Clients: Use this feature to focus on the clients you are currently responsible for, minimizing distraction from unrelated tasks.

3. Utilize the Filter Icon on the To-Do List

To enhance your task management:

  • Locate the Filter Icon: It is situated at the top right corner of the To-Do List screen.

  • Apply Filters: You can filter tasks to view:

    • People You Are Currently Working With: Focus on tasks related to your immediate clients.

    • Overdue Tasks: Identify and prioritize any overdue tasks to ensure timely completion.

    • Pending Tasks: Keep track of tasks that are upcoming or awaiting action.

4. Log Items Promptly

Timely logging of tasks is crucial:

  • Log as Soon as Practical: Ensure that you log any completed tasks, incidents, or updates as soon as possible to maintain accurate records and enable real-time tracking of care activities.

Best Practices

  • Stay Organized: Regularly review and update your To-Do List and Clients Tab to keep your workflow organized.

  • Communicate: Use the app to communicate with other team members, ensuring everyone is aware of task statuses and client needs.

  • Be Consistent: Develop a habit of checking the app at the beginning and end of your shift, and periodically throughout, to stay on top of your responsibilities.

By following these guidelines, you can maximize the efficiency of your care provision using the Log My Care app, ensuring our clients receive the best possible care.

For any questions or further assistance, please contact your supervisor or refer to the app's help section.

Thank you for your dedication and commitment to providing exceptional care.
